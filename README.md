Current games only show **one part** of the automotive world.

Automation focuses on engines.

Car Company Tycoon focuses on company management.

# What Makes This Game Different

This project isn't about driving.

It isn't just about management.

It isn't just about engineering.

It isn't just about racing.

It is about **creating an entire automotive ecosystem** where every decision—from the diameter of a piston to the layout of a factory, from an AI assistant in the dashboard to a Le Mans victory—ripples through the company and influences the next generation of vehicles.

The ambition is to let players experience what it feels like to be the founder, chief engineer, R&D director, manufacturing head, and motorsport leader of a world-class automotive company. Rather than offering isolated systems, the game aims to simulate the complete life cycle of an automobile and the evolution of the company behind it, making engineering choices meaningful, interconnected, and rewarding. This holistic vision is what sets your idea apart from existing automotive simulation games.
